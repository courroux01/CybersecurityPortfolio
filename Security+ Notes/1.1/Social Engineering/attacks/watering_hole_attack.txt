Watering Hole Attack
- your organization is "secure"
- bad guys can't simply get in
- no random clicks, hard to phish
- if you cant find the cave, find the water source instead!
- social engineering and Reconnaisance method
- determine which websites or places victims go
- poison that place in hopes of infecting multiple people or infecting deep enough to pivot to a weak point inside
- visiting certain sites might download malicious script files that would only trigger on certain events

Prevention
- defense in depth: layered defense
- firewalls, ips, anti-virus and malware
